Re: Re:Cycle

8 August, 2015

Welcome to Re:cycle. The purpose of this site is to create a space for cyclists who are trying to keep it light. This may be our hobby, but sometimes we commute by the merit of our own leg-strength. This may be our primary mode of transport, but we would still like to find some enjoyment in the sport. This is a space to discuss how to balance joy and purpose in a city where such a fun, healthy, even meditative activity can, at times, become political and even harrowing.
So join us. Re:duce the white noise. Re:use the enthusiasm you had when you first bought your wheels. Re:cycle.
