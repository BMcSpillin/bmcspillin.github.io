McMission

After graduating from a B.F.A. program at NYU, I found myself excelling in my field as a bartender. Having earned a spot in Time Out New York's Top 50 Bartenders of 2015, I knew it was time to move on and accept a new challenge. A desire to build and develop projects in the long-term had grown in me, and so I jumped ship, eager to excel in an industry that is constantly evolving, bringing along my penchant for people and my enthusiasm for efficiency. I am your creative, client-facing implementer. I am your self-starter and your team-player. I am McSpillin.
